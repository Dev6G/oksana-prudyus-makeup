About Oksana Prudyus

Professional Makeup Artist & Educator with over 10 years of experience in the beauty industry.

Oksana Prudyus is an internationally recognized makeup artist who has worked with celebrities, fashion shows, and editorial shoots. Her passion for beauty and education has led her to create comprehensive training programs that empower aspiring makeup artists worldwide.

With a unique approach that combines technical excellence with artistic creativity, Oksana has trained hundreds of successful makeup artists. Her courses emphasize not just technique, but understanding skin types, color theory, and the business of makeup artistry.
