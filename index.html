About Oksana Prudyus

Award-winning makeup artist and educator with a passion for empowering aspiring artists

Professional Photo

Your Journey to Makeup Mastery Starts Here

With over 15 years of professional experience in the beauty industry, I've had the privilege of working with celebrities, bridal clients, editorial shoots, and fashion shows worldwide. My journey has taken me from humble beginnings to becoming one of the most sought-after makeup artists in the industry.

Now, I'm dedicated to sharing my knowledge and techniques with aspiring makeup artists. My comprehensive training program is designed to take you from beginner to professional, providing you with the skills, confidence, and business knowledge needed to succeed in this competitive field.

My teaching philosophy combines technical expertise with artistic creativity, ensuring that each student develops their unique style while mastering industry-standard techniques.
